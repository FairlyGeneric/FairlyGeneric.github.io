Personal Profile

Myers-Briggs Test

Link to test

Result: INFP

This reflects me as I am often a more reserved and quiet person, unless around peers that I am more comfortable with. I prefer a smaller, tight knit group of friends over a large, but less involved circle of friends. I have a set of personal values that I generally adhere to when making judgements, and I try to see the intent behind things. I’m likely to be more open minded in groups. This suggests that I work best in a small group of colleagues, as I can come to be more familiar with each of them, and be open to various different options for potential issues in the future.

Learning Styles Test

Link to test

Results:
  • Auditory: 25%
  • Visual: 50%
  • Tactile: 25%

This suggests that I am a visual learner. In a team, this means I would work best drawing up diagrams and various other visual aids to both understand concepts and communicate them more effectively. It’s best to form a team with people that have a similar learning style, but also to include people with different learning styles as the test would suggest that Auditory and tactile leaning styles make up the remaining 50%.

Creative Problem Solving Test

Link to test

Result: Openness to creativity - 88

A result of 88 indicated that I have a high tendency to approach problems with a degree of creativity. In a team, I am more likely to be open to a variety of different solutions from different people, and seek to find different ways to approach things. It’s best for me to form a team of people who are all very different to each other, in order to produce aforementioned variety into the problem solving process.
